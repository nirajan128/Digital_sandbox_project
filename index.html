TECH TITANS

Nirajan Shrestha, Guri, Ajay, Prince

Teams Driving question:
What digital tools can student use to enhance thier learning,personal and working skills?

Adobe Acrobat

An Adobe tool that can read(view) any files and write edit PDF files or convert any files to files

Adobe Acrobat is a very useful tool for students. It lets you turn documents into PDFs, which keeps everything neat and looking the same when you share them with others. You can highlight important parts and add notes to your PDFs, which makes studying and reviewing easier. Filling out forms and signing documents digitally is simple and quick with Acrobat. You can also combine different notes and papers into one file and organize them in any order you like. This makes it easy to keep all your study materials together. Working on group projects is smoother because you can share your PDFs and add comments together in one place. Overall, Adobe Acrobat helps students manage their schoolwork better and makes many tasks easier to handle.
Animoto

A cloud-based video creation tool that produces videos using images, video clips, music, and different elements.

Animato is great for students because it helps make creative and fun presentations. It allows them to work together on projects even if they're in different places. Making videos with Animato helps students explain ideas clearly. It also improves their storytelling and speaking skills. Plus, using Animato teaches them important tech skills. Overall, it's a great tool for making learning more interesting and interactive.
Google form

An application used for creating forms for data collection purposes including surveys, and quizzes

Google Docs is very helpful for students in many ways. It allows them to write and edit documents from anywhere with an internet connection. Students can collaborate by working on the same document at the same time, making group projects easier to manage. The auto-save feature ensures that their work is never lost, providing peace of mind. They can add comments and suggestions, which is great for peer reviews and teacher feedback. Additionally, Google Docs offers many tools for formatting and organizing their work, helping them create polished and professional-looking documents. Overall, Google Docs makes studying, writing, and collaborating much easier and more efficient for students.
